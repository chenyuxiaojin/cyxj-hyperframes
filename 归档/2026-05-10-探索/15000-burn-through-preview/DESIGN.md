# $15,000 烧穿 Design

## Style Prompt

赛博朋克黑金金融灾难片。深空黑底、低机位 3D 透视网格、金色财富粒子、血红警示条和崩盘 K 线噪声。整体不是克制科技风，而是末日金融大片：昂贵、危险、过载、烧穿。

## Colors

- Deep space black: `#0A0A0F`
- Gold: `#D4AF37`
- Blood red: `#FF2D2D`
- Hot amber: `#FFC857`
- Bone white: `#F8F3E7`

## Typography

- Primary numeric font: `JetBrains Mono`, weight 700
- Secondary labels: `JetBrains Mono`, uppercase tracking

## Motion

- Act 1: 650 gold particles converge from all edges into the center.
- Act 2: number counts from `$0` to `$15,000`, with shake, blur, red crash bars, dollar parallax, and glitch flashes.

## What NOT To Do

- No Apple-style restraint.
- No soft pastel gradients.
- No default blue SaaS palette.
- No random or time-based rendering logic.
